How to Prepare Egg Masala

Description

The Egg Masala is one of my favorite dishes. Pair with rice or Chapathi, and you are good to go. It contains numerous spices and is garnished with Coriander. I feel like you could take this for Lunch or Dinner and it would turn out to be delicious.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Cumin seeds
  • Eggs - 3
  • Onions - 2
  • Tomatoes - 2
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Spices - Turmeric Powder, Fennel Powder, Chilli Powder, Coriander Powder, Garam Masala, Salt
  • Kasuri Methi (Also Known As Dried Fenugreek Leaves)
  • Coriander leaves

Preparation

  1. Hard boil your eggs and slightly fry them in oil, along with turmeric, chilli powder and salt, until golden brown.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds to it.
  3. When the cumin starts to pop, add onions and fry it for a few minutes, until it turns golden brown.
  4. Once the onion has turned golden brown, add the ginger-garlic paste and fry it.
  5. Once the raw smell is cooked off, add the pureed tomato to the mixture and cook it, till the oil separates.
  6. Add the spices: Turmeric powder, Fennel powder, Chilli powder, Coriander powder, Garam Masala and Cumin powder.
  7. If you want to down the spice, add some curd to the gravy to mellow it down.
  8. Add the fried boiled eggs, and garnish the gravy with some Kasuri Methi and Coriander leaves.
  9. The Egg Masala is now ready to eat.
