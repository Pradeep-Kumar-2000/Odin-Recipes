Channa Gravy

Description

The Channa Gravy is one of the most tastiest gravies and it is vegan. It is known as Chickpeas in the United States. It is truly mouth watering and a delight to prepare.

Ingredients

  • Soaked Chickpeas - 1 cup
  • Cumin seeds
  • Bay leaves
  • Cardomom seeds
  • Cloves
  • Cinnamon
  • Onions - 1 cup
  • Tomato Puree
  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Oil or Butter
  • Fresh cream
  • Chilli Powder
  • Garam Masala
  • Cumin Powder
  • Salt
  • Coriander Powder
  • Kasuri Methi
  • Coriander leaves

Preparation

  1. Heat oil in a pan, along with Cumin seeds, Bay leaves, Cardomom seeds, Cloves and Cinnamon sticks.
  2. As the added spices slowly start to pop, add the cup of onions and saute them for three to five minutes.
  3. As the onions start turning a bright golden brown, add the ginger garlic paste.
  4. After the raw smell of the ginger garlic paste mellows down, add the pureed tomatoes in the pan.
  5. Cook the onion-tomato mixture until a gravy like consistency appears and the oil separates from the gravy.
  6. Add the remaining spices: Chilli Powder, Cumin Powder, Salt, Garam Masala, and Coriander Powder.
  7. Cook it for five more minutes and add some fresh some cream to obtain a creamy consistency.
  8. In the end, add some Kasuri Methi and Coriander leaves as garnish.
  9. The Channa Masala or Channa Gravy is ready to eat.
